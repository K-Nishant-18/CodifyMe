## Visual Notes for Production
- **Color Palette:** Stark Black (#000000), Neon Green (#00FF00) or Hot Pink (#FF00FF), Off-White (#F0F0F0).
- **Typography:** Use monospaced fonts (Courier, Roboto Mono) mixed with massive sans-serif headers (Inter, Archivo Black).
- **Transitions:** Use "datamosh" or "pixel sort" transitions between scenes to emphasize the digital/tech vibe.
